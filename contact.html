FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about how we work.

⏱️

How long does a project take?

Timelines vary by scope. A typical landing page takes 2–3 weeks, while full platforms can take 2–4 months. We'll give you a clear timeline during our discovery call.

💰

What's the payment structure?

We typically work with a 40% deposit, 30% at midpoint, and 30% upon completion. Enterprise projects can be billed monthly or upon agreed milestones.

🔄

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! Our Starter plan includes 2 rounds of revisions, while Professional and Enterprise plans include unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied.

🤝

Can we meet in person?

Absolutely! We're based in San Francisco and happy to meet locally. For remote clients, we use Zoom, Slack, and Figma for seamless collaboration.

🛠️

What technologies do you use?

We work with React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, Flutter, AWS, GCP, and more. We choose the best stack based on your project's specific requirements.

📞

What about post-launch support?

Every project includes post-launch support ranging from 1–12 months depending on your plan. We also offer ongoing retainer agreements for continuous improvements.
