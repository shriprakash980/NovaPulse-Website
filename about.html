Our Story

From a Garage Startup to a Global Team

NovaPulse started in 2014 with a simple belief: technology should be beautiful, accessible, and powerful. What began as a two-person team working out of a garage has grown into a global organization of 50+ talented individuals.

Today, we partner with startups, scale-ups, and Fortune 500 companies to create digital experiences that users love and businesses depend on. Our work spans web applications, mobile platforms, AI integrations, and cloud infrastructure.
